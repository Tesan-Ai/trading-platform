ML TRADE BRAIN v1 — CURRENT STATE (NEEDS MAJOR IMPROVEMENT)
================================================================================

Location: ml_brain/
Model: scikit-learn LogisticRegression or RandomForestClassifier
Role: Filter/scorer ONLY — never places orders

Training flow:
  1. collect_labeled_candidates() walks historical minute data
  2. Strategy-approved setups get labeled:
       label=1 if target hit before stop on forward bars
       label=0 if stop hit before target
  3. 17 numeric features (VWAP distance, OR levels, RVOL, regime, time, returns)
  4. Time-based 70/30 train/test split (no shuffle)
  5. Saved to models/ml_trade_brain_v1/model.joblib

Train command:
  python ml_brain_runner.py train \
    --start-date 2025-09-03 --end-date 2026-06-03 --model-type logistic
